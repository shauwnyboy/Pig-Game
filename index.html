How To Play

Welcome to Pig, a game of chance and strategy. Test your luck and skill against an opponent as you race to see who can reach the winning score first!

  1. Player rolls the dice
  2. If the player rolls a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, the roll is added to the players current score
  3. If the player rolls a 1, their current score is set to zero and their turn ends
  4. If the player HOLDS, their current score is added to their total score, their current score is set back to zero, and their turn ends
  5. The first player to reach 100 total score is the winner
