How to Play

  • Player rolls the dice
  • If the player rolls a 2, 3, 4, 5, or 6, then the roll is added to the players current score
  • If the player rolls a 1, their current score is set to zero and their turn ends
  • If the player HOLDS, their current score is added to their total score, their current score is set back to zero, and their turn ends
  • The first player to reach 100 total score is the winner
